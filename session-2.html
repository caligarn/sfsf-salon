02 — Schedule

How the evening unfolds.

0:00 — 0:10

Opening & Reading Reactions

Quick round-the-room reactions to the suggested reading. What stuck with you? What felt prescient? What felt dated? Keep it tight — everyone gets a minute.

0:10 — 0:40

Resonating Themes

Open discussion: what sci-fi themes do we think are important to cover right now? What stories — books, films, shows, games — are resonating in this moment? What's missing from the conversation?

0:40 — 1:00

Breakout Conversations

Split into small groups to go deeper on the themes that surfaced. Each group picks one theme and discusses: why does this matter now, and what would a great story about it look like?

1:00 — 1:15

Group Report-Back

Each breakout group shares their top theme and story idea with the full room. Brief, focused — what emerged from the conversation?

1:15+

Open Social

Unstructured mingle time. Continue conversations, make connections, or head out — no awkwardness either way. The structured portion is done.
